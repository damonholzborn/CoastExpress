Coast Express

?

Welcome to Coast Express, a MIDI settings manager for the Make Noise 0-Coast.

Process

  1. Choose the MIDI port of your 0-Coast from the pull-down menu.
  2. Change settings.
  3. (Optional) Save settings on the 0-Coast: hold PGM A to enter the Program Pages, then hold PGM B to save.
